AHH! SPOOKY!


Monsters have invaded Game Jolt, and we need your help! Join forces with other Game Joltoids to defeat the waves of monsters!

How to play


Find monsters

Monsters are hiding all over the site. If you encounter one, click on it to begin combat! The monsters do not like to be poked and will try to avoid you, so be quick!


Gain ranks

Fending off different monsters will unlock achievements and ranks of honor that will be visible on your profile! Can you achieve the highest ranks?

Its a community effort


Community goals

Rumors have it there are around 5,000 monsters of each type! If all of them are defeated before it's too late, all participant's ranks and achievements will become permanent!


Rare? share!

Some monsters are quite rare, but you can help each other find more of them! When you find a rare monster like the dreaded vampire, link the page to your fellow Joltoids! Monsters hang around for a few minutes after being discovered for everyone to catch.

Global Stats

You can see how many monsters were defeated overall.
