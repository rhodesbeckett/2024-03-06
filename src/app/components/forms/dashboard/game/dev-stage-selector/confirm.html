When switching your game page from a {{ $ctrl.from }} page to a {{ $ctrl.to }} page, the follow changes will occur:

  • People will now be able to rate your game (unless you turn it off in your Settings).
  • Your ratings will reset and we will show a new rating average for your finished game page. Your early access ratings will be archived.
  • Your game rating will be reset to your early access rating. Your current ratings will be archived for when you want to transition back to a completed game page.
  • Ratings will be disabled for your game. Any ratings you currently have will be archived until you transition back to an early access or completed game page.
  • Public packages/builds for your game will now be visible to everyone.
  • Any public packages/builds for your game will be hidden while your game page is devlog-only.
  • Your game media will now be visible.
  • Your game media will be hidden.
  • Your game's devlog feed will be switched into its own tab, and you can continue posting devlog posts!
  • Your game's devlog feed will move back to your game's main page. There will be no separate devlog tab.
  • Your game will show again at the top of "new" listings.
